Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Sign up using your SDSU email address (@sdsu.edu) and create a password. You'll need to choose whether you want to use the app as a Student, Poster, or both.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account directly from within the Second Course app:

  1. Open the app and go to the Profile tab
  2. Tap on "Delete Account" at the bottom of the screen
  3. Enter your password to confirm
  4. Tap "Delete" to permanently remove your account and all data

Warning: This action cannot be undone. All your posts, photos, and claims will be permanently deleted.

I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password?" and enter your email address. You'll receive an email with instructions to reset your password.

How do I change my notification preferences?

Go to the Profile tab and you'll see notification preference toggles for your role (Student or Poster). Adjust them as needed.

How do I report a problem with the app?

Please email us at daniel@secondcourse.co with details about the issue you're experiencing. Include:

  • What you were trying to do
  • What happened instead
  • Any error messages you saw
  • The device and iOS version you're using

I'm having trouble with push notifications

First, check your device settings to ensure notifications are enabled for Second Course. Then, go to the Profile tab and check that your notification preferences are turned on. If you're still having issues, contact us with details.

Why do I need to allow photo library access?

The app needs access to your photos so you can attach images to your food posts. This helps other students see what food is available before they claim it. When you create a post, you can choose photos from your library or take new ones with the camera.

Account & Data

What data do you collect?

We collect your email address, display name, profile information, posts, and claims. For a complete list, please see our Privacy Policy.

How can I access or update my data?

You can update your profile information within the app. To access your data or request a copy, email us at daniel@secondcourse.co.

How can I request account deletion?

You can delete your account directly in the app (see instructions above) or email us at daniel@secondcourse.co to request deletion.

About Second Course

Second Course is a food sharing platform for the SDSU community. It connects students and faculty who have leftover food with those who want to claim it, reducing food waste on campus.
